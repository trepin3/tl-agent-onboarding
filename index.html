Step 2 of 6
Schedule Your Exam
Once you've completed your pre-licensing course, schedule your state licensing exam.
What You Need To Do
Schedule your Arizona Life Insurance Licensing Exam through Pearson VUE (the official testing provider for Arizona).
Steps
  • Create an account at pearsonvue.com
    Search for "Arizona Department of Insurance"
  • Select the Life Insurance exam
  • Choose a testing center near you and pick a date
  • Pay the exam fee
    ~$55
  • Bring valid government-issued photo ID on exam day
TIP: You need a 70% or higher to pass the Arizona Life exam. Take all the practice tests your pre-licensing course offers — the real exam is very similar.
Important: After passing, you must apply for your license through the Arizona Department of Insurance (DIFI) within 12 months or you'll need to retake the exam.

Step 3 of 6
Fingerprinting
Arizona requires electronic fingerprinting for all insurance license applicants. There are two parts: the DPS background check and the fingerprint appointment.
Part 1 — AZ DPS Background Check
Submit your background check application through the Arizona Department of Public Safety.
DPS Steps
  • Go to psp.azdps.gov
    Open AZ DPS Portal
  • Click "Background Check for Employment/Licensure"
  • Create an account (or log in if you have one)
    Select "Yes" → "Individual" → Fill out profile → Verify email
  • Once logged in, click "Background Check for Employment/Licensure" again
  • Enter fingerprint code:
    INS-055.A
  • Agree to Privacy Statement, select "Applicant" (or "Employee" if Applicant isn't available)
  • Complete applicant info, e-signature, and fingerprinting preference
    Select "physically present in Arizona" if you're in-state
  • Review, submit, and pay the $22.44 fee
  • Save your Reference Number (starts with "A")
    You NEED this for the fingerprint appointment. Download and save the receipt.
Important: Save your Reference Number! It starts with the letter "A" followed by numbers. You'll need it to schedule your fingerprint appointment.
Part 2 — Schedule Fingerprint Appointment
After getting your Reference Number from DPS, schedule your fingerprint appointment through Fieldprint Arizona.
Fieldprint Steps
  • Go to fieldprintarizona.com
    Open Fieldprint Arizona
  • Click "Schedule Appointment"
  • Sign up or log in
  • Scroll to "Don't have a Fieldprint Code?" section
    Select "Applicant Processing" → "Continue with this Reason"
  • Enter your DPS Reference Number, last name, and date of birth
  • Choose a fingerprint location, fill in your info, and pay the Fieldprint fee
  • Attend your appointment — done!
TIP: If you run into issues on the DPS portal, use the "Chat with us" button on the bottom right of their site. They're responsive and can help troubleshoot.

What You'll Need
  • Your NPN number (National Producer Number)
    You receive this after getting licensed. Find it at nipr.com
  • E&O Insurance (Errors & Omissions)
