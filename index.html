Step 1 of 6
Pre-Licensing Course
Complete your state-required pre-licensing education before you can sit for the exam.
What You Need To Do
Purchase and complete a state-approved Life Insurance Pre-Licensing Course for the state of your state.
Purchase Pre-Licensing Course — $29.99 →
TIP: Most pre-licensing courses can be completed in 1-2 weeks of dedicated study. Don't rush it — passing the exam on the first try saves you time and retake fees.
What You'll Study
Life Insurance Basics — types of policies, term vs. whole life, riders, beneficiaries

State Laws & Regulations — insurance code, DOI rules, consumer protection

Ethics & Market Conduct — licensing requirements, continuing education, agent responsibilities

Step 4 of 6
Get Contracted
Submit your contracting paperwork through SureLC to get appointed with our carriers.
What You Need To Do
Complete your contracting through the SureLC portal. This is how you get officially appointed with our carrier partners so you can write business.
Open SureLC Portal →
Carriers You'll Be Contracted With
Submit contracting for each of the following carriers:
American Amicable GTL Ethos
Additional carriers may be added as they become available.
What You'll Need
  • Your NPN number (National Producer Number)
    You receive this after getting licensed. Find it at nipr.com
  • E&O Insurance (Errors & Omissions)
    Some carriers require this before appointment
  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Voided check or bank info for commission deposits
TIP: Contracting can take 1-2 weeks per carrier. Submit all carriers at once to get appointed as quickly as possible.

Step 5 of 6
Set Up Agent Portals
Once contracted, set up your login for each carrier's agent portal.
What You Need To Do
Create your agent account on each carrier's portal. You'll use these daily to submit applications and track business.
Carrier Portals
  • American Amicable — Agent Portal
    Register at americanamicable.com once contracting is approved
  • GTL (Guarantee Trust Life) — Agent Portal
    Register at gtl.com once contracting is approved
  • Ethos — Agent Portal
    Register at ethoslife.com once contracting is approved
Note: You won't be able to set up portal access until your contracting is fully approved for each carrier (typically 1-2 weeks).
